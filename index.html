Welcome to PT Medika Mulya Utama

This website is a demonstration of network security using MikroTik firewall and DDoS protection with Cloudflare.

About Our Project

We aim to secure both internal networks and website infrastructure. MikroTik firewall protects internal network security, while Cloudflare safeguards our website from DDoS attacks.

Testing DDoS Protection

We will simulate a DDoS attack and monitor its impact before and after applying Cloudflare's protection.

System Overview

  • MikroTik Router: Securing internal company networks.
  • Cloudflare: Providing DDoS protection at the internet layer.
  • InfinityFree: Free hosting and domain for testing.
  • Slowloris: Used for DDoS attack testing.
  • Pingdom: Monitoring website performance.
